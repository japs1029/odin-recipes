Lasagna

Description

A classic Italian dish commonly made with ground beef or substitue layered in between gooey delicious pasta sheets with cheese fillings.

  • Beef
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Seasonings
  1. Mix beef and seasonings together until fully cooked
  2. Cook lasagna sheets as directed by package
  3. Mix ricotta cheese with seasonings and set aside
  4. In a deep dish pan, layer pasta sheets followed by ricotta filling and beef on top until all contents have been used
  5. In a 450 degree oven, bake with tin foil cover for 35 min
  6. Enjoy a delicious lasagna with friends and family
