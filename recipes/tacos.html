Lasagna

Description

A common Hispanic/ Latino dish with immense flavor in every little bite taken.

  • Beef/ Meat Choice
  • Small Tortillas
  • Cheese Blend
  • Cilantro
  1. Cook desired meat to perfection
  2. Heat up tortillas on skillet
  3. Top tortilla with meat, cheese, and cilantro
  4. Eat as many tacos as you can!
